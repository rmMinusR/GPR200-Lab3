User Input:

Space:			Move upwards globally
Left Control:		Move downwards globally
W:			Move forwards locally
A:			Move leftwards locally
S:			Move backwards locally
D:			Move rightwards locally
Escape:			Move camera to defaut position
Mouse (click & drag):	Rotate the camera
